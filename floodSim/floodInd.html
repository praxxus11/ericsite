About:

This is a non-recursive breadth and depth first implementation of floodfill. I first tried a recursive approach but the recursive function calls in javascript are a pain so I didn't want to deal with it.

Instructions:

First click on the red button to start the game. Then click on draw barriers to draw some walls, try to include holes and enclosed areas. Once all walls are drawn, try to pick one starting point. If you choose multiple, then the program will just choose the last one drawn. Once ready, click on your fill preference to start!

You can continue to draw initialization points after the filling is complete.
