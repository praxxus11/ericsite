Hybrid Viability Tool

This tool uses data from the ICPS Drosera genetics page to make a prediction. Based on the approximate chromosome count, ploidy, and approximate classification of two Drosera species, it creates a probablity model of offspring success.

Since the tool relies only on these two factors, it may not be completely accurate in its prediction. However, it does provide a rough baseline for seed viability. I am confident that it will rule out species that are guaranteeded to produce sterile seeds, such as a cross between odd-ploidy species.

Simply enter two species into the boxes below and click on predict.
